SMS Consent & Opt-In Policy

How Bright Automations collects written consent before sending any text messages.

Overview

Bright Automations uses SMS messaging to communicate with customers about their website builds, service updates, and promotional offers. We collect explicit, written opt-in consent from every user before sending any text messages. No messages are ever sent without the customer first checking the consent box on our checkout page.

How We Collect Consent

During the checkout process at brightautomations.org/checkout.html, customers must check a required SMS consent checkbox before they can complete their purchase. The checkbox is unchecked by default and must be actively selected by the customer. The "Pay Now" button remains disabled until both the Terms of Service and SMS consent boxes are checked.

Key: Consent is not a condition of purchase. The SMS checkbox is separate from the Terms of Service agreement. Customers are clearly informed they can reply STOP at any time to opt out.

Message Types

Customers who opt in may receive the following types of SMS messages:

  • Website preview notifications — alerts when their free website preview is ready to view
  • Build updates — status updates during the 48-hour website build process
  • Service communications — revision confirmations, launch notifications, billing reminders
  • Promotional offers — occasional offers for add-on services and upgrades

Opt-Out & Support

Customers can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any text message. After opting out, they will receive a single confirmation message and no further texts will be sent. Customers can also contact us directly to request removal:

  • Email: admin@brightautomations.net
  • Phone: (610) 256-9844

Privacy

Phone numbers collected through our opt-in process are used solely for the purposes described above. We do not sell, share, or rent customer phone numbers to third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
