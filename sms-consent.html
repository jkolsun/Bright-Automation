SMS Opt-In Policy

How we obtain consent before sending you text messages

Last updated: March 2026

How You're Added to Our SMS List

Bright Automations only sends text messages to people who have explicitly opted in through a form on our website. Here is exactly how our opt-in works:

  1. You visit a request form on brightautomations.org — for example our free-preview form (/preview) or our contact form (/contact) — and enter your business details, including your phone number.
  2. In that same form you check a dedicated SMS consent box that reads: "Bright Automations SMS Consent: By checking this box, I agree to receive recurring account and customer-care text messages from Bright Automations at the mobile number provided — website preview and build-status updates, launch and edit-status notifications, monthly report alerts, and support replies. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. See our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy."
  3. The SMS consent box is unchecked by default and is never required to use our service — you must check it yourself before the form will submit.
  4. After you submit the form with the box checked, you may receive a text message from Bright Automations (typically within 15 minutes) confirming that we've received your request and are starting your build.

We never add anyone to our messaging list without this opt-in. Checking the SMS consent box is entirely optional and is not a condition of purchase. Submitting your phone number without checking the box does not opt you in to any text messages.

What Messages You'll Receive

Once opted in, you may receive:

  • Website preview and build-status updates
  • Launch and edit-status notifications for your site
  • Monthly report alerts
  • Replies to support questions you send us

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 5 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP at any time to immediately opt out of all future messages. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

For help, reply HELP or contact us at admin@brightautomations.net or (814) 400-2017.

Your Privacy

We will never sell, share, or rent your phone number or opt-in data to third parties for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for details.
