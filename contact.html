FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive automation projects may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your pricing?

Our packages include design, development, testing, deployment, and initial support. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Ongoing maintenance and support plans are available separately.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer various support and maintenance plans to keep your website and automations running smoothly. This includes updates, security patches, content updates, and technical support.

Can you integrate with my existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrations with popular platforms like CRMs, email marketing tools, payment processors, and more. We'll work with your existing tech stack to create seamless workflows.

What if I need changes after launch?

Changes are easy! Existing clients can use our Client Portal to submit change requests. We prioritize client requests and typically implement minor changes within 24-48 hours.
