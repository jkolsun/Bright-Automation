Our Story

How It All Started

Bright Automations was born from a friendship forged at Pennsylvania State University. As students pursuing our passions in technology and business, we recognized a shared vision: to make powerful digital solutions accessible to businesses of all sizes.

We saw too many businesses struggling with outdated websites, manual processes, and missed opportunities. Combining our expertise in development, AI, and operations, we set out to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and everyday business needs.

Today, we're proud to bring that same collaborative spirit and innovative mindset to every project we undertake. From small retail shops to professional service firms, we're committed to being the technology partner that helps our clients thrive.
