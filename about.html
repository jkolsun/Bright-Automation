Our Story

How It All Started

Bright Automations was born from a friendship forged at Pennsylvania State University. As students pursuing our passions in technology and business, we recognized a shared vision: to make professional web presence accessible to every business owner.

We saw too many home service businesses — plumbers, roofers, HVAC techs — losing jobs because they didn't have a decent website. Or they were paying $3,000+ for one. We knew we could do it better: professional sites, built fast, at a price that actually makes sense.

Today, we bring that same drive and builder mentality to every site we launch. If you're a home service business that needs a website that works as hard as you do, we're your team.
